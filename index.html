Boston Terrier

What they look like

Boston Terriers also called a Boston bull are compact and well-muscled dogs. They are short with a square like appearance for a head but it is proportional to the rest of its body. It has a black nose, slightly undershot bite, large round dark eyes, and most are born black and white some are born brown and white.

What they act like

Boston Terriers love to learn and without the proper training it can become rambunctious and high strung. They are easy to train and pick up things quickly but be careful not to let it develop small dog syndrome. The Boston terrier has a life expectancy of 15 years and grow up to 17 inches and weigh up to 25 pounds. They are very friendly and are hard to housebreak.

More Information

The boston Terrier is the 23rd most popular dog breed it's coat is very short and fine with moderate shedding. The most common coat colors it has are black, brindle, seal or a combination of the three over a white background. The rarest colors are liver, cream, red and brown. The white on the body of the Boston Terrier should cover the chest, muzzle and neck. There should be a white blaze that reaches from the muzzle towards the top of the head, but should not touch the eyes.
